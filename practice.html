paragraph is a distinct unit of writing that consists of one or more sentences focusing on a single topic or idea.

It serves to organize thoughts and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow along.

Paragraphs typically begin on a new line, often with an indent, and can range in length from a single sentence to several, but they should be long enough to fully develop the central thought without overwhelming
